# Code of Conduct

## Our commitment

We are committed to making participation in the ToraDB community a harassment-free experience for everyone.

## Expected behavior

- Be respectful and constructive.
- Assume good intent and communicate clearly.
- Welcome newcomers and different viewpoints.
- Focus feedback on ideas and implementation, not people.

## Unacceptable behavior

- Harassment, discrimination, personal attacks, or intimidation.
- Trolling, insulting/derogatory comments, or disruptive conduct.
- Publishing others' private information without permission.

## Scope

This Code of Conduct applies in project spaces, including issues, pull requests, discussions, and community channels associated with ToraDB.

## Reporting

If you experience or witness unacceptable behavior, report it privately to project maintainers through your repository's private contact channel.

Include:
- what happened
- where/when it happened
- links/screenshots if available

## Enforcement

Maintainers may take any action they deem appropriate, including warning, temporary restriction, or ban from project spaces.
